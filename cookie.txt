Ingredients:
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cups packed brown sugar
- 1/2 white sugar
- 3/4 STICK (6 T.) butter, melted
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup cranberry rasin
- 25g raw sunflower seeds

Directions:
1. Melt butter in a bowl.
2. Whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl.
3. Stir butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until well combined.
4. Add in eggs and vanilla extract to butter and sugar mixture, and stir to combine.
5. Add in cranberry rasin and raw sunflower seeds, and mix in until evenly distributed.
6. Cover the mixture bowl with plastic wrap, and put in refrigerator for 20 minutes.
6. Preheat oven to 325.
7. Scoop out 2 T. of dough per cookie, and roll cookie dough into balls. Place on parchment paper lined baking sheet.
8. Bake for 15 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
